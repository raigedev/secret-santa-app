# Product Context: My Secret Santa

Register: product

## Product Purpose

My Secret Santa helps friends, families, and small teams run private gift exchanges with less coordination friction. The app handles groups, wishlists, recipient reveals, gift progress, reminders, private messages, and shopping ideas that can use affiliate links while keeping the experience friendly and trustworthy.

## Users

- Gift exchange participants who need to know who they are gifting, what that person wants, the gift day, and the group budget.
- Group owners who create exchanges, manage members, run assignments, and keep the event moving.
- Returning users who may belong to several active or concluded exchanges.

## Product Principles

- Keep every workflow clear, private, and calm.
- Use festive personality only when it supports the task.
- Prefer shopper-facing language: recipient, wishlist, group budget, shopping idea, gift progress, reminder, message, report.
- Preserve affiliate, auth, and ownership safety. UI polish must not weaken private data boundaries or click/postback tracking.
- Avoid duplicate navigation paths. Each primary sidebar item should lead to a distinct, useful workspace.

## Brand And Tone

Warm, playful, and reassuring. The product should feel like a polished holiday tool, not a generic admin dashboard or a noisy marketing page.

## Anti-References

- Generic SaaS cards everywhere.
- Neon, purple-blue AI gradients, glass panels, or decorative clutter.
- Backend or provider jargon in customer-facing screens.
- Screens that look identical after clicking different nav items.
